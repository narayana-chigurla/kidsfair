Mother & Baby Zone

Mother & Baby Zone is specially designed to provide an ideal platform for manufacturers to establish contact with the decision-makers from the wholesale and retail trade chains and hospitals. People from various segments of the maternity, baby & kids’ industry visit and network, gaining exposure to potential business opportunities and leads.

Potential for Market Growth

Due to rapid urbanization, a growing child population, and changing consumer preferences and needs, the Indian market for maternity, baby, and kids care products is growing significantly. The projected growth rate of the market has been more than 10% annually and the market is expected to grow at the rate of 13%.

Major segments for growth:

  • Baby Food
  • Baby apparel
  • Baby diaper and wipes
  • Baby skincare
  • Baby toys
  • Baby accessories

The market for maternity, baby and kids products in India has witnessed a spurt in usage and production. There is a wide scope for investment in this segment that’s been broadening horizons for growth and development in the field.

Focus Areas - Mother & Baby Zones

Baby Care

Baby care comprises of product range - Baby Skin Care, Baby Hair Care, Baby Toiletries, Baby Wipes, Baby Monitors and others.

Feeding & Nursing

A space for products such as Baby Bottles & Teats, Breast Pumps, Breast feeding accessories (such as Breast Care products and Specialty Feeding Devices) Feeding Bottle Care, Pacifiers, Sippers, Tableware, Food Storage and so on.

Diapering

It will showcase all kinds of diapers-Cloth Diapers, Disposable Diapers, Bio-degradable Diapers, Diaper Bags, Changing Tables, Diaper Pail, and others.

Carrier

A designated for the Baby Transport items, Pushchairs/Strollers, Car Seats, Baby Slings, Carrycots, Portable Bassinets, Carriers, Higha Chairs and Accessories.

Nutrition

Nutrition products ranging from Baby Food, Kids Nutritional Supplements, Organic Food Items, Dried Baby Food, Milk Formula, Prepared Baby Food etc.

Fashion

Apparels for baby & kids, Bibs, Footwear, Accessories, Organic Baby Wear and Kids Wear, Maternity Wear & Accessories, etc.

Toys

Encompasses all kinds of Games & Puzzles, STEM and STEAM-based toys, Infant and Preschool Toys, Dolls, Construction Toys, Outdoor & Sports, and others.
